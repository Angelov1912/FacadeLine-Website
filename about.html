Who Are We?

Facade Line is in the trade since 2013. It was founded by Viktor Nikolov, who is an experienced window fitter and he is in the industry more than 10 years. Facade Line has started its journey in the construction sector as a small company with just a few dedicated professionals and over the last few years, it has grown to include design, supply as well as fit products in the glazing and cladding industry, both for commercial and residential buildings. Top quality with personal attention to your needs. If you have any questions about Curtain wall, Cladding, Windows & Doors, check our services or use our contact page.
